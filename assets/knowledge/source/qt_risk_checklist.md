# QT / TdP Risk Checklist

## High-risk signals
- QTc >= 500 ms
- Rapid QTc increase from baseline
- Low K and/or low Mg
- Multiple QT-prolonging agents
- Bradycardia
- Structural heart disease (e.g., CHF)
- Renal dysfunction raising drug exposure

## Safer action framing
- Correct electrolytes first
- Review necessity of QT-prolonging agents and consider alternatives
- Repeat ECG after interventions
